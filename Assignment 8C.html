About director

Hayao Miyazaki is a Japanese animator, director, producer, screenwriter, author, and
manga artist. A co-founder of Studio Ghibli.He has attained international acclaim as a
masterful storyteller and creator of animated feature films, and is widely regarded as
one of the most accomplished filmmakers in the history of animation.
